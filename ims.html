IMS Enhancement: Key Strategic Upgrades

Revolutionizing Inventory Management for Efficiency and Accuracy

July 5, 2025 Sanidhya Kumar Session

Meeting Minutes & Key Takeaways

Today's session with Sanidhya Kumar laid the groundwork for significant advancements in our Inventory Management System (IMS). We're moving towards a more dynamic, automated, and insightful approach to stock control. The following outlines the critical decisions and the tangible benefits we expect from these implementations.

1. Streamlined Stock Updates: Focus on Flow

The Change:

Instead of manual daily stock counts, we will update the **closing stock once**, and thereafter, our daily operations will focus solely on recording **total daily purchases (inwards)** and **total daily outwards**. This shifts the focus from static inventory checks to dynamic flow management.

What We Get:

  • **Real-time Accuracy:** Our IMS will reflect the true closing stock at the end of each day automatically, based on the day's transactions.
  • **Reduced Manual Effort:** Significantly less time spent on redundant daily stock tallies.
  • **Improved Data Integrity:** Minimizes human error associated with multiple stock updates.
  • **Clearer Transactional View:** Provides a precise record of daily inflow and outflow for better analysis.

2. Dynamic Average Daily Consumption (ADC)

The Change:

Our ADC will no longer be a static number. It will be **dynamically calculated based on the last 'X' days of outward movements**, where 'X' can be configured (e.g., 60 days, 90 days, or any custom period). This makes our consumption patterns responsive to recent trends.

What We Get:

  • **Smarter Forecasting:** ADC adapts to seasonal changes, market demands, and sales trends, leading to more accurate reorder points.
  • **Optimized Stock Levels:** Prevents overstocking or understocking by understanding true consumption rates.
  • **Reduced Holding Costs:** By aligning stock with actual demand, we minimize unnecessary inventory carrying costs.
  • **Enhanced Planning:** Better insights for production, procurement, and sales strategies.

3. Automated Stock Level & Safety Factor Adjustments

The Change:

An **automation will be built to constantly monitor closing stock for the last 30 days**. In the latter case (overstocking), it will **automatically adjust the safety factor** based on the percentage difference from the Max Level.

What We Get:

  • **Proactive Issue Detection:** Identifies potential stock-out risks or overstocking situations before they become critical.
  • **Self-Optimizing Inventory:** The system learns and adjusts safety stock levels, reducing manual intervention.
  • **Minimized Capital Lock-up:** Prevents excessive capital tied up in slow-moving or overstocked inventory.
  • **Improved Service Levels:** Ensures critical items are always available, reducing missed sales opportunities due to stock-outs.

4. Enhanced Owner's Dashboard: Crystal-Clear Inventory Visibility

The Change:

Owners will gain access to a powerful new dashboard feature providing **searchable, filterable, and detailed inventory insights**. This view will allow them to check **any item's stock on any specific date or across a date range**, with options for category-wise filtering and more.

What We Get:

  • **Instant Business Intelligence:** Owners can make faster, data-driven decisions regarding inventory.
  • **Transparency & Accountability:** Full visibility into inventory movements and levels across the organization.
  • **Strategic Planning:** Better understanding of stock patterns to inform purchasing, sales, and operational strategies.
  • **User-Friendly Access:** Intuitive interface designed for quick and efficient data retrieval.

5. Precise Lead Time Calculation

The Change:

We will implement a system to **automatically calculate the Lead Time for each item**. This will be defined as the duration **from the Purchase Order (PO) generation date to the PO Acknowledgement date** by the supplier.

What We Get:

  • **Accurate Supplier Performance Metrics:** Objective data on how quickly suppliers acknowledge orders.
  • **Improved Procurement Planning:** Better predictability for order fulfillment and inbound logistics.
  • **Negotiation Leverage:** Data-backed insights to discuss and improve supplier lead times.
  • **Reduced Stock-Out Risks:** More reliable lead times contribute to more precise reorder scheduling.

Looking Ahead: A Smarter, More Efficient IMS

These strategic enhancements mark a significant leap forward in our inventory management capabilities. By leveraging automation, dynamic calculations, and enhanced visibility, we aim to:

  • **Minimize Inventory Holding Costs:** By optimizing stock levels.
  • **Prevent Stock-Outs:** Ensuring product availability to meet customer demand.
  • **Boost Operational Efficiency:** Reducing manual tasks and improving data accuracy.
  • **Empower Data-Driven Decisions:** Providing stakeholders with timely and accurate insights.

We are committed to building an IMS that is not just a record-keeping tool, but a strategic asset driving our business growth.
